Datasets

(All data is aggregated into 5 minute intervals.)

CNR
Carrier to Noise Ratio. An average of CNR data where Status is 1. (A Status value of 1 indicates with high probability that the lidar recorded the laser signal rather than background noise.) To further reduce noise, I also counted every range/time combination with 3 or fewer Status==1 measurements as missing data.
DRWS
Dispersion Radial Wind Speed. Calculated with the same method as CNR.
Horizontal Wind Speed, Vertical Wind Speed, x Wind Speed, y Wind Speed
Average of wind estimates made by combining the radial wind speed estimates from 5 consecutive scans in DBS mode. All 5 consecutive measurements must have a Status of 1 for a wind estimate to be recorded.
Wind Direction, wind barb overlay
Average horizontal wind speeds (same as the wind speeds above). For legibility I'm only showing the values at 30-minute intervals and I'm averaging heights in 250m bins.
Temperature, Vapor Density, Liquid, Relative Humidity
Averages of the corresponding microwave radiometer datasets from the 'Zenith' processor where quality is 1.
